My interests in photography, graphic design, and the culinary arts motivated me to find the union of my technical and creative skills.

Culinary Arts

I was drawn to the freedom and intensity of the creative process of visualizing a meal on a plate.

After graduation, I completed a craftsmanship training at the Indian Institute of Hotel Management, Catering Technology and Applied Nutrition, Mumbai.

​After my training, I worked as an apprentice at The Table, Mumbai, to hone my culinary skills.
