ABOUT ME

Who am I ?

Hi, I am Pratik Mangtani, a product developer and designer, who takes an interdisciplinary approach to design. I have a background in computer engineering. I plan to advance my self-taught journey in design by designing solutions that use information to empower people.

What I do?

My goal is to bring empathy to technology. My background in Computer Science enables me to bring a technical point of view to design in addition to advocating for users' needs.

To fulfill these goals, I contribute to both product development and UX design through my work.

How the culinary arts made me a better designer?

Apart from technology, I am passionate about the culinary arts.

My desire to design technology that enhances users’ experience stems from my background in the culinary arts and computer engineering. Culinary arts and Human-Computer Interaction both work towards the common goal of creating a stimulating experience for people. Just like I arduously plan ingredients to create dishes, I break down problems and tailor individual modules to curate effective solutions for customers.

This led me to discover the union of my technical and creative skills through HCI.
