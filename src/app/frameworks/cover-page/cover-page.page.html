ABOUT ME

Who am I ?

Hi, I am Pratik Mangtani, a product developer and designer, who takes an interdisciplinary approach to design. I have a background in computer engineering. I plan to advance my self-taught journey in design by designing solutions that use information to empower people.

What I do?

My goal is to bring empathy to technology. My background in Computer Science enables me to bring a technical point of view to design in addition to advocating for users' needs.

To fulfill these goals, I contribute to both product development and UX design through my work.
