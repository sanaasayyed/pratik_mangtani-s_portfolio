ABOUT ME

Who am I

Hi, I am Pratik Mangtani, a product designer and developer, who takes an interdisciplinary approach to design. I have a background in Computer Science and the Culinary Arts. I plan to advance my journey in design by designing solutions that use information to empower people.

I am currently studying MS in Human-Computer Interaction at The University of Michigan.

I like to think of myself as a full-stack product designer who can lead a project from research and design to the prototyping phase to ensure a smooth developer handoff.

Team Table
